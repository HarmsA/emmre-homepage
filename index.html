Learn More

Easier than texting

Type what you need to done into the message bar just like you are going to send a message, then press the “send as task” button. A task has been created, assigned, and sent to the other person!

Pressing the thumbs up automatically messages back that the task has been seen and accepted.

Click the task complete and a message is automatically sent, giving full visibility of the task without ever leaving your chat window.

Updates at the press of a button

Wanting an update on a task? Just press the request update button. The request is automatically messaged to the other person with full context and information about the task.

To ensure the quickest response, simply reply to that message. The update is threaded and stored with the task to ensure nothing is missed and everything is documented.

Full task management suit

Emmre has everything you need to ensure full visibility so all of your tasks get done

Easily assign tasks to each other or both of you. Set due dates and reminders for yourself or each other. Put tasks into projects or tags to keep everything organized.
